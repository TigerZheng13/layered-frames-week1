Studio Overview

Layered Frames explores the art of video production, editing, and visual effects using Adobe Premiere Pro & After Effects. Students develop essential filmmaking techniques — from cinematic storytelling, lighting, and sound design to motion tracking, AI-assisted visuals, and advanced compositing.

Through practical projects, you'll learn to construct visual narratives that articulate your own perspective and creative vision — exploring how digital media becomes a tool for storytelling and world-building in contemporary filmmaking.

What You Will Learn

🎞️

Develop proficiency in video production, editing, and post-production using Premiere Pro and After Effects

💡

Apply cinematic storytelling — lighting, framing, and sound design — to build compelling visual narratives

✨

Explore VFX workflows — motion tracking, compositing, and AI-assisted visual techniques

🌍

Cultivate a critical understanding of how digital media serves storytelling and world-building
