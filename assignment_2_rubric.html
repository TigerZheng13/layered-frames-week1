FilmCinematography & Composition

10%
HD
Excelling
Outstanding control of framing, composition, and camera movement across both live and AI footage. Director thinking is consistently evident — depth, motivated lighting, parallax movement where appropriate. Technical choices feel intentional and cohesive throughout.
D
Advancing
Competent framing and composition across most shots. Some director thinking visible (depth, deliberate camera movement). Live and AI shots are mostly aligned visually. Minor inconsistencies but overall positive impact on spatial storytelling.
C
Developing
Some compositional intent but uneven execution. Camera movement may rely on AI defaults (zoom, pan) without considered direction. Live and AI shots may not always feel cohesive. Landscape mostly maintained.
P
Commencing
Limited compositional thought. Most shots feel like AI defaults or static documentation. Live and AI footage are visibly disconnected. Camera and framing show minimal intentionality.
F
Unsatisfactory
Cinematography appears arbitrary or absent. Shots show no compositional thought. Orientation choices severely affect clarity. Little evidence of learning.

FilmEditing & Rhythm

15%
HD
Excelling
Editing powerfully shapes the viewer's experience. Pacing breaks at emotional turning points (master → insert → cutaway pattern is visible). AI long takes are broken and re-rhythmed. Rhythm changes feel deliberate and emotionally charged. Cut placement creates a cohesive whole.
D
Advancing
Editing is generally purposeful with effective pacing. Some scenes use insert cuts and rhythm breaks well. AI footage is mostly well-trimmed. Some moments may feel uneven, but overall structure supports the narrative.
C
Developing
Editing is functional but uniform. AI footage often plays at original generated length without rhythmic reshaping. Sequence is understandable but doesn't actively shape experience. Few rhythm breaks or inserts.
P
Commencing
Editing is minimal or poorly considered. Long uncut AI clips dominate the film. Relies on a single rhythm throughout. Little variation in pacing.
F
Unsatisfactory
No meaningful editing evident. Film feels like raw AI output or unstructured assembly. No rhythm choices visible.

FilmAI Spatial Imagination

25%
HD
Excelling
AI content is creatively conceived and clearly serves the spatial story. Prompts demonstrate strong director thinking — motivated light, considered staging, narrative anchoring, foreground occlusion or atmospheric depth where appropriate. AI-generated worlds feel imagined and personal, not generic. The transformation between real and AI space is meaningful.
D
Advancing
AI content shows clear creative intent and serves the story well. Prompts demonstrate developing director thinking. Some moments may feel generic, but overall AI content is well-conceived and personal.
C
Developing
AI content is present but feels generic or "demo-like." Prompts read as descriptive rather than directorial. AI generations may feel like mood-board outputs rather than scenes from a film.
P
Commencing
AI content lacks clear creative intent. Generations feel arbitrary or randomly chosen. Prompts show minimal sophistication. AI feels like tool-led output rather than director-led creation.
F
Unsatisfactory
AI content is absent, poorly chosen, or used in ways that contradict the spatial concept. No evidence of prompt craft or directorial thinking.
